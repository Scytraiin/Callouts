# Callouts v1.1.0

New feature: **Trigger Suggestions**. No config migration required from v1.0.x.

## Suggestions tab (`/callouts suggestions`, 💡 toolbar button)
Callouts now watches your fights and proposes ready-to-adopt triggers, ranked by how
much they matter — then lets you create a rule in one click.

- **Aggregates the interesting stuff**: recurring enemy casts and the debuffs landing on
  you and your party, grouped into categories and ranked.
- **Smart ranking**: long casts, AoE mechanics (circle/cone/line from the action data),
  and debuffs-on-you score highest; auto-attacks and self-buffs (e.g. Well Fed) are
  excluded. Each suggestion shows a hint like "4.0s cast · Circle 8y" or "debuff · 9s".
- **One click to adopt**: **Create rule** opens the editor pre-filled; **Copy import
  code** puts a shareable rule string on your clipboard; **Ignore** dismisses a
  suggestion for good (persisted).
- **Per encounter**: switch between "This fight" and "This session"; the last pull is
  kept after a wipe. Optionally auto-open the tab when combat ends (Settings).
- **Advanced tier**: with advanced sources enabled, head markers (spread/stack/…) on you
  or your party show up as named suggestions too.

Everything stays local and read-only, exactly like the rest of the plugin.

## Notes
- Suggestion data is session-only (never saved); only your ignore list is persisted.
- AoE-shape labels and the marker mapping are best-effort and improve with in-game tuning.
